Request for Proposal (RFP)

Youth Arts & STEM Initiative

Funder: The Civic Progress Foundation
Location: National (USA)

Funding Goals

The Civic Progress Foundation seeks to fund innovative after-school programs that bridge the gap between arts and STEM education for underserved youth (ages 12-18).

Key Priorities

  • Programs that demonstrate a clear link between creative expression and technical skill development.
  • Initiatives that serve communities with limited access to enrichment activities.
  • Projects with measurable outcomes related to student engagement and skill acquisition.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Applicant must be a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.
  • Proven track record of at least 2 years in youth program management.
  • Proposed project must serve at least 50 students.
